ABOUT

VIT was founded with the objective of offering top-notch higher education that meets international standards. It continually strives to enhance the quality of higher education through innovative methods. The campus boasts a diverse student population from all over the world, and is staffed by well-educated and knowledgeable teachers who are dedicated to nurturing the students. At VIT, striving for excellence in teaching and research has become a way of life. The motivated and talented student body is a source of pride. Our partnerships with various international universities through Memoranda of Understanding have greatly benefited us. These agreements allow for student and faculty exchanges and joint research projects between the universities. Our students who conduct research abroad bring both quality and recognition to their work, and bring honor to India. We will continue our progress with steady determination. We look forward to welcoming you to VIT.

HISTORY

It was established under Section 3 of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act, 1956, and was founded in 1984 as a self-financing institution called the Vellore Engineering College. The Union Ministry of Human Resources Development conferred University status on Vellore Engineering College in 2001. The University is headed by its founder and Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, a former Parliamentarian and Minister in the Tamil Nadu Government. In recognition of his service to India in offering world class education, he was conferred an honorary doctorate by the West Virginia University, USA. Mr.Sankar Viswanathan, Dr.Sekar Viswanathan and Mr.G.V. Selvam are the Vice-Presidents; Dr. Rambabu Kodali is the Vice-Chancellor and Dr. Partha Sharathi Mallick is the Pro-Vice Chancellor I/C.
